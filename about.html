About Us

Our Journey Delicious Delights was founded in 2010 by M. Hassan, Umer Jameel and Zia-ud-din with a passion for creating delicious and authentic meals. We started with a small pizzeria in WahCantt and quickly gained a loyal following for our fresh ingredients, handmade dough, and unique flavor combinations. Over the years, we've expanded to 3 locations across Rawalpindi, but our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains the same We use only the freshest ingredients, source locally whenever possible, and take pride in our traditional techniques.

Our Mission Our mission is to provide our customers with the best meals experience possible. We believe that food is more than just a meal, it's a way to bring people together and create lasting memories We strive to create a welcoming and friendly atmosphere where everyone feels at home.
